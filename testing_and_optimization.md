# Testing and Optimization

## Testing Goals
The goal of testing was to observe the RC car behavior and improve the overall system performance.

## Main Test Areas
- Motor response
- Steering behavior
- Driving stability
- Vehicle movement
- System reliability
- Mechanical balance

## Test Observations
During testing, the RC car behavior was checked under different movement conditions such as forward movement, turning and stopping.

## Optimization Focus
The optimization focused on improving:
- Smooth vehicle movement
- Stable steering
- Better motor response
- Reliable system operation
- Mechanical and electrical integration

## Engineering Learning
This testing process helped connect theory with practical mechatronics work. It showed how mechanical design, electronics and motor control affect the final behavior of a vehicle system.
